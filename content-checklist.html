Content Checklist

A few things that would make your new website even stronger. Nothing here stops the site going live, these are optional additions you can send us whenever you're ready.

Everything you supplied is on the site. Your logo, your photo and all the page content you uploaded have been used. The points below are extras that would help your site convert more visitors into enquiries.

Things to confirm

End of Tenancy Cleaning

You listed End of Tenancy Cleaning as one of your services, but the written page content only covered Domestic, Commercial, Weekly and Office cleaning. We have written a short, general description for it. Please confirm you offer this service and send any specifics you'd like us to add (for example, what's included, or whether it's aimed at tenants, landlords or letting agents).

Insurance & checks (a big trust signal)

Cleaning customers strongly value knowing a company is insured and that its cleaners are vetted. If you have public liability insurance, or your cleaners are DBS-checked or reference-checked, this is one of the most powerful things we can add. Please only confirm what is genuinely in place, and we'll add it to the site. We have not assumed any of this.

Phone or WhatsApp contact

Right now the site invites enquiries by email and through the contact form only, as you requested. If you'd like to add a phone number or WhatsApp button later, just send it over and we'll add it across the site.

Business address

The site describes you as serving London and the surrounding areas, with no fixed address shown. If you have a registered business address you'd like included (for example, in the privacy policy or footer), send it over. This can also help with local search visibility.

Ways to strengthen the site

  • Reviews. The Reviews page is ready and honestly invites feedback, but it has no customer quotes yet. As you complete jobs, ask happy clients for a short review (and set up a Google Business Profile so reviews appear in Google). Send us any you receive and we'll add them, this is the single biggest booster for a cleaning site.
  • Real photos. The current images are professional stock-style photos chosen to match your brand. Real photos of your team at work, or before-and-after shots of jobs you're proud of, would make the site feel even more genuine. Send them whenever you have them.
  • Pricing guidance. There's no pricing on the site yet, which is fine for a quote-based service. If you'd like to add a "from" price or an hourly guide, it can help visitors self-qualify before enquiring.
  • Areas you cover. If you focus on specific London areas or boroughs, listing them (or adding a short "Areas we cover" section) helps people find you in local searches.
  • Social links. If you have Facebook, Instagram or a Google listing, send the links and we'll add them to the footer.

What we looked at

To shape your site, we reviewed how established London cleaning companies present themselves. The strongest ones consistently lead with reliability and trust, show insurance and vetting badges, make quotes easy to request, and use real reviews as social proof. Your site already leads with reliability and makes quotes easy, adding genuine reviews and any insurance or vetting details (above) would complete the picture.

Ready to send anything over?

Reply to us with any of the items above and we'll update your site. You never have to wait until you have everything, send bits as they come.
